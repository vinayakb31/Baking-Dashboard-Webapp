Our Story

Bakelet was born from a passion for simple, honest baking. We believe that the best flavors come from the best ingredients, patience, and a lot of love.

The Art of Slow Baking

We don't rush things here. Our sourdough ferments for 48 hours to develop its complex flavor and airy texture. Our croissants are laminated by hand, layer by layer.

Located in the heart of the city, we aim to be a warm gathering place for the community. Whether you're grabbing a morning coffee or picking up a treat for a special occasion, we want every visit to Bakelet to be a moment of joy.
